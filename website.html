Introduction

Group 4 - April 15,2022

With this new age of the internet and people becoming reliant on technology, society is becoming more at risk then we ever have been. All of our important information is slowly being put online, especially with the pandemic.

Whether it is just a credit card number for food delivery, an account password, or your whole identity, that information that could make or break a person is online and there are bad people that will stop at nothing to get it. How do you prevent it? System security of course. By knowing and understanding these few things about system security, you are already a lot safer than not.

Firewalls are one of the many well known ways to protect a system. They let only the good data by and turn away anything potentially dangerous.

Antimalware is a type of software program that protects information technology (IT) systems and individual computers from malicious software (malware). These programs scan a computer system to prevent, detect, and remove malware.

Passwords are characters that allow access to a computer system. They are the first line of defense against unauthorized access.

Encryptions take your information and turn it into a binary code to protect it is important to have.
